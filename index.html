Projects

Sword Fights on the Elevator

This is your average sword fighting game, but with a twist. There are multiple swords, each with their own special ability. Dive into it and challenge other foes to sword duels in order to earn money. You can spend your gold in the shop to buy cosmetics. So, do you have the courage to dive into the arena and make a name for yourself?

Project: Doors Cenat

This is a thrilling first-person horror experience where players must reach Door 100 while avoiding deadly entities. You can start with up to 4 players, navigating rooms filled with unique dangers. The path is uncertain, with temporary refuges and hidden threats. Valuable items, from coins to useful tools, can make or break your journey.

Project: Kai Cenat

Project: Kai Cenat is a work in progress game scripted by Xatilore, built by GaitPlayz and animated by Bogdanol. In this game, you defend yourself against waves of zombies by placing down towers, each with their own unique abilities. There aren't many maps yet, but there's certainly plenty of towers. I wonder though... who's the final boss?

Apeirophobia Wiki

This wiki covers the Roblox horror game Apeirophobia, inspired by the Backrooms Creepypasta. The game features many levels, each with puzzles and terrifying entities. Trapped and desperate for escape, you must navigate these treacherous levels. Will you endure or break under pressure? Let’s hope for the former.
